Pano Open

Saturday, January 28, 2012

 

Sitting at the bump jam apres wednesday, and I get a page from Alli:  “If pano was opening tomorrow, would you want to know?”  Gunter made some turns up there on thursday, and we got a decent dump on friday (with the top closed!).  So, Saturday rolls around, I drop off Max, and head straight there.  Good timing...there was a queue, and they JUST were opening the lift.  I made about the 30th chair.


The top was windy and windblown, but the trees were good.  Did laps through Johnson’s, Willet’s, and Kinnikinic, then decided to see if the upper switchyard trees were open.  They were, and it was glorious!  But, after hearing about the accident last weekend, after one lap, I decided I shouldn’t be doing those alone, and headed to pioneer. 


Gunter then gives me a page, and I head back over.  We did lap after lap over there...good stuff!!  Really reminds me how much I love the powdery trees!!!


Other (newly) open terrain:  Pine Cliffs (yay!), Super Gauge Trail (enabling the Franken).  Pine Cliffs was hard-packed, but nice to be able to ski something with some pitch to it.  Still closed:  Runaway and the Chutes, Brian’s, Stagecoach & Sundance, Larkspur, Sky Pilot, Fireberry, and all of Eagle Wind.
