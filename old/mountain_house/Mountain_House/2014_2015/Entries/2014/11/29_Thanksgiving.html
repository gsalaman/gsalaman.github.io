Thanksgiving

Saturday, November 29, 2014

 

4” snow monday, 2” tuesday, and 8” wednesday means that WP could finally open the top.  Headed out Wednesday...they had Cranmer, all the Olympia stuff, and Bash and Balsh open.  Still a little sketchy at the bottom of Balsh, but hey, real moguls in November?  Woo hoo!  And got this cool shot of Max jumping into Cheshire:

 

Then on friday, Jane opened.  They had Jane Trail, Frenchy, Gandy, Lower Arrowhead, Rainbow Cut (including the bumps at the bottom that probably *shouldn’t* have been open) and the bottom parts of the Iron horse blacks.  Hi-Lo also opened.  Oh, and Retta’s and Outrigger opened as well.  Friday was just me and Alli...and of course she had to wear the dress.  Saturday was a Dad and Max day...had to hit more moguls!
