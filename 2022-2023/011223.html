Belle Opens!!!

Thursday, January 12, 2023

Some new snow yesterday, but winds closed down Pano and Eagle early. So, even though we didn't get much overnight, I broke out the S7s with plans to hit the trees under Pano and over at Eagle. Went up Pano, and had this view:

Plenty of untouched caches this am in the trees:

So headed to Eagle; did a lap through Left Hand. As I was getting ready to hit the traverse, I notice the Mayor! Rode the lift with him; he told me they just opened Belle!!! As I went by on Eagle, it was closed, so it *just* opened....and yeah, I had to head straight there. Road at the top was sketchy...yeah, I got a dinger from a rock. But the pow in the trees was AWESOME!!! No pics (other than that top entry one)...but hopefully there will be many more runs down there this season!!!
