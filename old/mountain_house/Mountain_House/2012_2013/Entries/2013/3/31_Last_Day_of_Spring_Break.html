Last Day of Spring Break

Sunday, March 31, 2013

 

Last day before Gunter had to go back (and I had to hit work).  Definitely spring conditions...icy mornings and slushy afternoons, so we got a late start.  Brought out both the new Rocker2’s and the S7s...but only used the S7s.  Started with a couple warm-up laps  under the C-lift, including Gunter doing Hole-in-the-Wall.


Ended up hiking the cirque *twice* today...once to pick up the west headwall, and once over on Destiny, where we continued through to waterfall.  Cool stuff back there...but a little slushy.  Can’t wait to try it sometime when there’s less-baked snow!


Also nabbed Belle and Brian’s Run.   Good stuff...but yes, I’m sore!!!
