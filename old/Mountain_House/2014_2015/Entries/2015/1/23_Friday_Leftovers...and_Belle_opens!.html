Friday Leftovers...and Belle opens!

Friday, January 23, 2015

 

It’s been dry since the break...not a lot of snow to speak of.  Then, this week, WP got 6” on Monday night, 5” Tuesday night, and another 3” Wednesday night.  I went up solo Thursday night for a day of skiing Friday, searching for leftovers.


Started over at Eagle.  Not too bad...the Thunderbird trees were much better than Medicine Man & Black Coal.  You could squeeze over to Left Hand...pretty good in there too; but I needed a water break.   Headed down the switchyard trees (REALLY good in there), and decided to do a Pano lap on the off chance they’d opened Belle...and they did!
