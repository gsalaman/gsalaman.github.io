So earlier in the season, we had an instance where Max and I going down Mary Jane Trail, and I want to do Gandy Dancer.  Max isn't up for it, so I show him where (I think) he needs to wait for me to come out.  I do the bumps, look around, and notice that spot is *way* above me on the mountain...and there is Max, faithfully waiting.  I call to him, but he can't hear me from that far away.  I look up, and consider hiking it....nah.  I ski down, take the lift, and meet him.  "Daddy, you took a *really* long time!" We agree at that point that it's better to ski with the 2-way radios.

Here comes the defining moment...later in xmas break.  I've got on my bandits, and Max and I are darting through the tree paths under where the timberline lift used to be. I'm following him...he makes a sharp left turn into the powder.  My bandit submerges, and the ski pops off (common occurrence....I'm gonna need to get something a little wider/longer for my default ski).  I dust off the snow, and pull out the radio.   "Max, Daddy fell...wait for me".  Wait.  Wait.  Get up.  Call him again.  "Daddy, I fell too!   My ski came off"  Oh crap...now I've gotta hike and find him and help him get his ski back on in the powder.  I ski down a little to look up for him.  Go down to this spot..."I can see you!  Do you need me to come up?"  "Nope...I got my ski back on".  And down he comes...

My little rockstar!
