Thanksgiving Break

Friday, November 29, 2013

 

Headed up to Fraser on Thursday after the big meal.  No new snow to speak of, but WP opened Jane on Tuesday.  We all headed up to the hill friday morning; Alli broke out the new dress!  Vid Below.

 

On Friday, they had the Pony, Goose, and Super Gauge spinning, with Frenchy, Gandy Dancer, Narrow Gauge, Sterling, Spike, and Arrowhead open.  You had to cut across from about 1/3rd of the way down to hit Spike and Arrowhead (avoids that top nasty stuff)...decent bumps in there though.  They also had the upper oly stuff open and Hilo open, but no eskimo and no oly lift...which means no cranmer.  Weird to have Jane open without Cranmer, but hey, I like that order better!  They also opened Mulligans, Bash, and Balsh mid-day.

Headed out solo on Saturday.  No changes on the Jane side; they opened up the Oly, Eskimo, Looking Glass, and Prospector.  Cheshire Cat was good...once you got past that top lip.  Under Prospector, only Jack and Butch...but you could take Mock Turtle around to the Oly.  Still no Cranmer...but once again, that’s not a bad thing.

Sunday I worked on my bumps...getting better!  They had Little Pierre open on the Park side...good, soft mini-moguls there.  I think my favorite was cutting high to arrowhead...good pitch, good snow up in there.
