Xmas 2004

For the second year in a row, we spent xmas up in the mountains skiing with the Stephens family (Rod, Michelle, Amy, and Ken) and Gunter.  This year's desitination:  the YMCA's Snow Mountain Ranch, with skiing at Winter Park.  A word of advice...although Gunter is a fun-lovin' guy, he tends to take you down "evil" slopes on the mountain.  He took Alli and me through a hellatious tree-trail "short-cut" from the top of the Zephyr Lift, cutting across 2 big black bump runs until finally depositing us on the Mary Jane trail.  He also convinced me to go down Derailer...a VERY long big bump run down the Mary Jane side.  (Note to self:  If Gunter sez: it's okay, it's groomed, check with an alternate source of info).

This did turn a co-worker's theory true: after doing volkswagon bumps, the little bumps aren't nearly as bad.
