Gunter Weekend #1

Sunday, January 22, 2012

 

Gunter came out for an extended week now that the terrain is getting better.  We’re gonna do the bump jam next wednesday.


They’ve opened Trestle and some more of the Challenger runs...long haul/short haul, needle’s eye, and boiler.  Cannonball was open on saturday, but not sunday.  Stagecoach is still closed...wind blown!!! 


Got some snow saturday night.  They were forecasting 8-12, but WP only got 5.  Still, better than nothing.  Got out early, and Jane was a madhouse!  Parked at Corona, skied down to Super Gauge, and the singles line was back beyond the ropes!  Did a quick lap through Edelweiss (and cut through the trees to get the pow below pano), but quickly went to Pioneer. 


Saw Matthias, Lucille, and Roland over there.  Did *MANY* loops there; Alli, Gunter, and Max joined us.  Alli quickly went off to find Lucille; Max, Gunter, and I went powder hunting.  Good stuff in the Buckaroo trees (but make sure you turn at the road, no matter *what* Gunter says).  Did living room, the weather station, brakeman trees, and wildwood before Max and Gunter bailed.  Met up with Matthias again...took him into lower Topher’s, and did Needle’s Eye.  He bailed back to the base; I did one more run through the brakeman trees.
