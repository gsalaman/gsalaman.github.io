Back to Mountain House

 
 
 

This all started at Alta.  At the Goldminer’s Daughter, they had a full scale topo map of the mountain made out of white cardboard.  It sat on a table, probably 4x4.  No trails, just all white...and I thought that was pretty cool.  I thought I could do the same thing with winter park, using topo maps, and gluing together cardboard layers.


Then, Max goes to the lego fest.  Hmmm...fits into my geek background much better...I can use legos to do the layers!  And then, the obsession truly began...
