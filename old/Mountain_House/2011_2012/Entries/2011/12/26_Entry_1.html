Copper Cruisers

Monday, December 26, 2011

 

Günter and I did a run to Copper on Monday...figured that if it was cruiser day, we’d go someplace with more stuff open.  They definitely had more terrain than winter park, but still, mostly just cruisers.  The bumps were in about the same troughed-out rocky condition. 


It was a little more crowded than earlier in the season, so I wasn’t able to go full throttle on some of the runs, but still, respectable speeds. :)
