More snow

Sunday, December 14, 2014

 

Between Thanksgiving and Sunday, we didn’t get any flakes.  Saturday was day 2 of Devo Travel Team for Max with Coach Scott...Alli stayed home, and I went and worked on my moguls.  They had C-lift and Sunnyside open...under C-lift was sketchy (Sleeper, Little Ten, Freeriders, and Phantom).  Same for Sunnyside (Bluebell, Roundhouse, and Edelweiss).  Got some good work in though...charts below.  Oh, and the perfect wax test:  see if you can make it from Super Gauge, down past Olympia and uphill to the top of Cheshire/Jabber without skating.

And then we got pounded on Sunday.  Initial report said 6”, but it kept increasing as the morning went on.  You could do the cut over above the Ice-Cut to Derailer, and Needle’s Eye was *epic*...pockets above my knees!!!  Living Room trees were decent, and the HiLo trees were fun as well (that’s the pic above).  Took a tumble in the trees left of Phantom though...did this number on my pole and ski:
