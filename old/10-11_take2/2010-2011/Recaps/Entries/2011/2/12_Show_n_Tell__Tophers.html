Show ‘n Tell: Tophers

Saturday, February 12, 2011

 

Alli had rugby this weekend, so, to continue the tradition, Gunter came out for “boys weekend”.  Max had class, so I had to do some show ‘n tell, and show Gunter the Skis and the plaque for Tophers.


This is a legendary underground area at Jane.  The story goes that Topher hit a tree well and died, and to mark it, they put his skis on the tree where he bit it.  There’s also a plaque you can see from Corona way if you look hard.
